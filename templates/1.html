What is Flux Balance Analysis?

A mathematical method for examining the movement of metabolites through a metabolic network is called flux balance analysis (FBA). An organism's metabolism is a process that keeps a series of reactions going in order to support it. A metabolic pathway can be used to describe all of these chemical processes in an organism. FBA calculates the flow of metabolites via this metabolic network, making it possible to forecast an organism's growth rate as well as the flux values needed to boost metabolite production in an organism.
