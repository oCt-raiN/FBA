Flux Balance Analysis

Flux balance analysis(FBA) is a mathematical approach for analyzing the flow of metabolites through a metabolic network.


Stoichiometric Matrix

Metabolic reactions are represented as a stoichiometric matrix (S) of size m × n. Every row of this matrix represents one unique compound and every column represents one reaction.


Constraints

Constraints are represented in two ways,
1) Equations that balance reaction inputs and outputs.


Optimal value

The next step in FBA is to define an ‘objective function’ that indicates how much each reaction contributes to the phenotype.
